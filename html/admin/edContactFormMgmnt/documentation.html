Contact Forms Management ------------------------------------------ -- Displays list of Contact Forms with it's Contact Persons. -- You can assign more than one Contact Persons for one form. -- All the contact persons of a form will receive email when user submits the form. -- You will be able to Add/Edit/Delete this information according to your rights. -- If the Contact form is deleted, all report data of that form will also be deleted. Contact Form Reporting ---------------------------------- -- Displays total no of forms submitted for a contact form. -- By default it displays current month's statistics. -- You can select the date range and Contact form name to filter the report. *********************************************************************************************************
